## Convert a standard campaign into a triggered campaign

Repurpose your standard campaigns by converting them into triggered campaigns, saving time and resources.

Written by Gareth Burroughes Updated over 3 weeks agoTo convert a standard campaign into a triggered campaign:

1. Go toCampaigns>Email.
2. Find and selectthe campaignyou want to convert. This opens the campaign in EasyEditor.
3. Go to theCampaign detailspage by selectingCreateorBack.
4. SelectCampaign settings.
5. UnderType, selectTriggered.
6. SelectApply.
7. SelectSave & continueorSave.Your converted campaign now appears inAutomation>Triggered email.
