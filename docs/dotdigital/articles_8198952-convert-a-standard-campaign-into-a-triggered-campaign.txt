Convert a standard campaign into a triggered campaign
Repurpose your standard campaigns by converting them into triggered campaigns, saving time and resources.
Written by
Gareth Burroughes
Updated over 2 weeks ago
To convert a standard campaign into a triggered campaign:
Go to
Campaigns
>
Email.
Find and select
the campaign
you want to convert. This opens the campaign in EasyEditor.
Go to the
Campaign details
page by selecting
Create
or
Back
.
Select
Campaign settings
.
Under
Type
, select
Triggered
.
Select
Apply
.
Select
Save & continue
or
Save
.
Your converted campaign now appears in
Automation
>
Triggered email
.
