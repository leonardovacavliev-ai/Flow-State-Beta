## Email campaigns overview

An email campaign is an email that you create, store, and send from your account.

- Previewyour campaign to see how it looks on different devices.
- Test sendyour campaign to see how it will look in different email clients and different devices.Alternatively, you canpay for an inbox and spam filter testfor an extensive set of results.
- Trackyour campaign to see how it performs after it's been sent.
- Sendyour campaign to different lists or segments.
- Translateyour campaign or its blocks into 39 languages using Dotdigital AI.

## Types of email campaigns

You can create the following two types of email campaigns:

### Standard campaign

A standard campaign is sent manually, either immediately or scheduled for a later time.

A standard campaign is the only type of campaign that can besent as a split test(also known asA/B testing).

### Triggered campaign

A triggeredcampaign is sent automaticallywhen an action or certain criteria are met, for example:

- When contacts reply to your campaign, you cansend them an automated campaign as an auto-responder.
- When contacts opt in to a list, you cansend them an automated campaign as a join action.
- When contacts opt in to a list, you cansend them an automated campaign as a confirmation email.
- When contacts reach a node in the program builder, you cansend them an automated campaign through the campaign node.
- When contacts click on specific links in your campaigns, you can automatically trigger targeted follow-up emails tailored to their interests.If you'd like to automatically send one of your standard campaigns, you canchange its type from standard to triggered.

## Personalise content in email campaigns

We offer the following options for sending personalised content to contacts:

- Dynamic content: Displays personalised content for contacts depending on the values of their contact data fields.
- External dynamic content: Displays personalised content that's served from an external server.
- Liquid: Displays personalised content, using a subset of Liquid markup language.

### Send email campaigns in different languages

As well as our default character encoding of Unicode (UTF-8), which is best for Latin-based characters, we also offercharacter encodings that support other languages.
